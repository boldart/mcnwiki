MCNi Wiki : Audio Player Feature

Created by Stephen Smith on Oct 24, 2018

Description

This feature embeds an audio player on band sites. Some or all public pages can contain a launcher, which opens a new browser window containing an audio player. Bands may upload songs and modify the player through management interfaces. The audio players will generally use XML to populate themselves.
