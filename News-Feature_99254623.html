MCNi Wiki : News Feature

Created by Stephen Smith on Oct 24, 2018

Description

This feature allows bands to post news items to their page. News items can appear on the front page, and they have a designated news page that may or may not be listed in the navigation bar. There is also a mangement interface.

News items have a title, date posted, author, and body.
