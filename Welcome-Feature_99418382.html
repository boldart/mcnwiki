MCNi Wiki : Welcome Feature

Created by Stephen Smith on Oct 24, 2018

Description

This feature prompts users to complete tasks to finish setting up their sites. Tasks are considered completed based on the status of the database. Individual tasks can also be ignored.
This feature also creates a site setup wizard. New users can be prompted to complete tasks one at a time and then hide all future site setup prompts.
