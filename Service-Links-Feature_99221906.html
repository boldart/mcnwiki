MCNi Wiki : Service Links Feature

Created by Stephen Smith on Oct 24, 2018

Description


Bands can add buttons to most content allowing fans to share the page on various social networking pages.

Currently, the list of supported sites is not customizable by the band and includes:

Del.ico.us
Digg
Facebook
Google Bookmarks
Google Buzz
MySpace
Ping.fm
Reddit
StumbleUpon
Twitter

This settings is configured under Settings > Social > Universal Settings
