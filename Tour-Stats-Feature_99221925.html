MCNi Wiki : Tour Stats Feature

Created by Stephen Smith on Oct 24, 2018

Description

Bombplates sites do some rudimentary tracking of bands' finances and contacts. Users can enter their income and expenses per show. They can also add contact information for significant persons and associate them with shows.

Note: This system needs substantial overhaul. No one is using the stats tracking parts because they are not very well-made. A few users are using the contacts.

These settings can be reached from either Tools > Tour Stats OR Content > Tour > Tour Stats
