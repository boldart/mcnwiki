MCNi Wiki : status endpoint

Created by Stephen Smith on Oct 23, 2018

The status endpoint allows a service to query the general status of the site.

Path: /status

Authentication: standard

Arguments: none

Result:

  • site_name - string - The name of the site (usually the band name)
  • site_description - string - the "Search engine sentences" from the settings form. Defaults to "The official $band_name band website"
  • current_status - Suspended|Pre-launch|Invalid site|Active - The current status of the site
