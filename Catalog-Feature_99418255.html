MCNi Wiki : Catalog Feature

Created by Stephen Smith on Oct 24, 2018

Description

This feature allows bands to post a catalog. A designated catalog page is created and can be linked in the navigation bar. A management interface is also created. Bands may add albums to the catalog, and songs may be added to the albums. The relationship between songs and albums is many-to-many. Albums contain many songs, and a song can be on many albums. Bands may also forego creating albums and just songs directly to the catalog. The catalog may be password-protected, much like the EPK.

Each song can also have a title, description/advanced details, tags (describing its genre, voicing, and tempo), and an mp3 preview.

Each album can also have a title, a release date, artwork, and other description/details using the WYSiWYG editor.
