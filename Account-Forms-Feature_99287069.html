MCNi Wiki : Account Forms Feature

Created by Stephen Smith on Oct 23, 2018

Description



Various forms exist on bombplates.com that allow users and account managers to manage sites. Bands have one site associated with their account. They can launch, finalize, and delete it. Account managers have many sites associated with their accounts. They can launch, finalize, delete, and transfer ownership of sites.
