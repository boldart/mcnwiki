MCNi Wiki : Image Slider Feature

Created by Stephen Smith on Oct 24, 2018

Description

This feature gives bands control over the primary image on their front page (and possibly other pages, depending on theme).

They can choose to leave the default image that their designer chose for them.

They can choose to upload a single image of their choice. It will be resized appropriately.

They can choose to upload a series of images, which will be displayed as a slideshow. Each of these images can be a link.

Also called Main Image.
